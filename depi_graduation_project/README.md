## ğŸ“– Overview  

**Guidee** is an intelligent mobile application designed to help users explore tourist attractions in **Egypt** in an organized and convenient way.  
Through a simple interface and an interactive map, users can view nearby attractions, add them to their visit list, read reviews from other visitors, and receive notifications reminding them of upcoming visits and travel plans.

The main goal of the app is to **enhance the tourism experience in Egypt** by organizing trips and providing reliable information in one accessible platform.

## ğŸ¯ Objectives  

- Facilitate the process of exploring tourist attractions for users.  
- Provide accurate details, high-quality images, and reviews for each location.  
- Enable users to create and manage their daily trip plans.  
- Send notifications reminding users of upcoming visits or suggested destinations.  
- Offer a smooth and fast user experience with **full Arabic and English language support**.
